1954 Mercedes-Benz 300SL

The 300SL accomplished much on its release. It started an elegant and suble style that continued into the late 50s and soon the 60s. It popularized gullwing doors. Seen on many supercars today, the doors open upwards. The 300SL had no room left for conventional doors.

Achievements

  • Fastest production car at the time capable of 163 MPH
  • Considered as the first super car
  • Reliable while being performance oriented
  • Inspired subtle yet beautiful body lines in design
