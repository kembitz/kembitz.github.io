1983 Ferrari Testarossa

The Testarossa is another example of Ferrari nailing car design. The Testarossa embodies the pop up headlights iconic to cars in the 80s. It inherits the Countach's low wide stance with a twist of Ferrari elegance. Ferrari stuck with sleeker corners that would quickly put boxier designs to shame. The Testarossa is an example of why Ferrari is thought of first when mentioning the best looking cars of all time.

Achievements

  • One of Ferrari's most successful race cars
  • Won multiple Le Mans races in France
  • One of the most produced Ferraris at 10,000 units made
  • Its wide rear hunches are kept in supercar designs today
